# Guide: LocalBusiness is the SME quick win

For a small business, the highest-ROI structured data is a `LocalBusiness` block with a consistent name, address and phone (the same NAP you use on Google Business Profile and directories). Get that right and validate it here, then confirm eligibility with Google's Rich Results Test on the live URL.
